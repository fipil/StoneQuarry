Stone Quarry — Standalone Repacked

The Stone Quarry mod by DArkHekRoMaNT, repacked for Vintage Story 1.22 without the CommonLib dependency.

Why this repack?

It appears that DArkHekRoMaNT currently doesn't have the time or motivation to update his mods for VS 1.22. We love this mod — it's fantastic and we want to keep using it on our server. That's why I decided to make this repack.

I'm aware that there are already two forked repacks on ModDB by TiMMyyMMiT and Kurallier. Thank you both for your effort and work! Unfortunately, both of those repacks are done in a way that makes them incompatible with the original mod — meaning if you already have quarried stone blocks on your map, you would lose them. You would also lose them later when (hopefully) DArkHekRoMaNT returns to modding and I can happily archive this repack and go back to his original mod.

Features

  1. Compatible with the original Stone Quarry mod — if you have quarried stone blocks from VS 1.21 on your map, you won't lose them.
  2. Compatible with the original Stone Quarry config file.
  3. No CommonLib dependency — you don't need it to run this repack. I extracted all the parts of CommonLib that Stone Quarry needs and brought them directly into the mod. The reason is that as Stone Quarry repacks appear, various repacks of CommonLib appear as well, and I want to eliminate user confusion and the question "which version of CommonLib does this work with?". This repack simply doesn't need CommonLib.
  4. Works in both Single Player and Multiplayer.

Disclaimer

Once DArkHekRoMaNT returns to modding and revives his original version of Stone Quarry, I will archive this mod and encourage everyone to switch back to the original. The mod is compatible, so you shouldn't lose your quarried stones — in theory :-)
